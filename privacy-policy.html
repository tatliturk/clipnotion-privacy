Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What data we collect

ClipNotion does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. All data stays on your device.

  • Your Notion OAuth token is stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local.
  • Your selected database and workspace name are cached locally for performance.
  • No analytics, no tracking, no third-party data sharing.

How we use your data

The Notion OAuth token is used solely to communicate with the Notion API on your behalf — to list your databases and save content you explicitly choose to save.

Third-party services

ClipNotion communicates only with the official Notion API (api.notion.com). No other third-party services are used.

Data deletion

You can disconnect your Notion account at any time from the extension settings. This removes all locally stored tokens and data.
