Privacy Policy – ShopSpy

Last updated: March 2026

What data we collect

ShopSpy does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All analysis happens locally in your browser.

  • No user accounts required.
  • No analytics or tracking.
  • Store data fetched from public Shopify endpoints is never sent to our servers.

How it works

ShopSpy reads publicly available data from Shopify stores (e.g. /products.json) directly in your browser. This data is displayed to you and never stored or transmitted elsewhere.

Third-party services

ShopSpy does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.
